Guard Rail Solutions

Roadway safety starts here—explore steel beam, cable, high-tension, and more.

Why Choose Guard Rails?

Our guard rails protect vehicles, pedestrians, and infrastructure by:

  • Absorbing and redirecting impact energy
  • Reducing vehicle rollover risk
  • Withstanding harsh weather and corrosion
  • Offering customizable attachment and transition options

Our Guard Rail Types

Steel Beam Guide Rail

W-beam and Thrie-beam sections for standard roadside protection.

Cable Guide Rail

Flexible multi-strand cables with wood or steel posts—ideal for scenic areas.

High-Tension Cable Rail

Extra-strong, pre-stretched cables for high-impact zones.

Crash Cushions

Impact attenuators to protect hazards at roadway ends and exits.

Ontario's Safest

Ontario-approved security rails featuring W-beam and Thrie-beam sections for maximum impact protection.

End Terminals

Smooth, energy-absorbing transitions between guard rail sections.
