Chain Link Fencing Styles

Durable, versatile, and cost-effective — discover all the chain link options we offer.

Why Choose Chain Link?

Chain link fencing is a popular choice for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. It offers:

  • Exceptional durability and low maintenance
  • Quick and easy installation
  • Cost-effective security solution
  • Options for privacy and aesthetic enhancements

Our Chain Link Styles

Galvanized

Standard galvanized steel finish provides corrosion resistance and a classic silver look.

Vinyl Coated

Available in Black, Brown, or Green. Provides extra weather protection and blends with landscaping.

Privacy Slats

Insert PVC slats into the mesh for up to 90% privacy — ideal for backyards and commercial perimeters.

High-Security

Heavier gauge wire and smaller mesh openings for critical security applications.

Diamond Mesh

Decorative diamond-pattern mesh for a more refined appearance without sacrificing strength.

Tension Wire Top

Stainless steel tension wire at the top rail for added rigidity and security.
