About Us

Serving Northeastern Ontario since 1979.

M & G Fencing Inc. is a full-service manufacturing, retail, distribution, and installation company. We process cedar posts for farm fencing and highway guide rails, serve all cash-and-carry customers with fencing materials, and supply contractors with a large variety of highway products.

Whatever the application—residential, farm, commercial, or industrial—our installation division delivers professional workmanship and ensures your project is done right the first time.

Our Commitment

Honesty and integrity are the cornerstones of our business. Backed by over 40 years of experience, you can expect expertly installed, high-quality products and personalized advice.

We know personal attention is as important as product quality, and we strive to provide both on every job—from blueprint consultation through final inspection.

Free Estimates

Our sales representative will meet with you to review your plans and offer expert advice on materials, layout, and budget. We’ll help you finalize every detail before work begins.
