Elegant Wrought Iron

Handcrafted for beauty, built for strength: choose from ornate panels, classic pickets, or full board privacy designs.

Why Choose Wrought Iron?

  • Timeless Beauty: Intricate designs add curb appeal and property value.
  • Durability: Solid steel construction resists impacts and weather.
  • Low Maintenance: Powder-coated finish prevents rust and fading.
  • Customization: Scrollwork, finials, and gates tailored to your style.
  • Security: Height and picket spacing deter intruders without blocking views.

Board-on-Board w/ Iron Topper

Full-seclusion vertical wooden boards for privacy, capped by decorative wrought-iron spear finials for added security and style.

Maintenance & Care

Wrought iron is built to last, but a few simple steps keep it looking new:

  • Wash with mild soap and water annually.
  • Inspect for chipped paint; touch up with matching powder-coat paint.
  • Lubricate hinges, latches, and automated opener points every 6 months.
