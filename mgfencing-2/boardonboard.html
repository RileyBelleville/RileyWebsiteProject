Board-on-Board Privacy Fence

Tight-seal vertical boards for total seclusion. Choose cedar, treated pine, or low-maintenance composite.

Why Choose Board-on-Board?

  • Complete Privacy: Overlapping boards eliminate gaps and sightlines.
  • Durability: Cedar and treated pine resist rot and insects; composite lasts even longer.
  • Noise Reduction: Solid construction dampens street and neighbor noise.
  • Customizable Finish: Natural stain, paint, or pre-finished composite colors.
  • Security: Solid barrier deters trespassers and pets stay contained.

Treated Pine

Pressure-treated pine boards offer strength and affordability with a solid privacy wall.

Board-on-Board w/ Lattice Top

Vertical overlapping boards capped with a decorative lattice for extra height and style.

Maintenance & Care

  • Wash annually with mild soap and water.
  • Inspect boards and rails; replace any damaged pieces promptly.
  • Re-stain or seal wood every 2–3 years to preserve appearance.
